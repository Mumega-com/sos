# Audience: Agencies

## Who They Are
WordPress agencies, digital marketing firms, SEO consultancies. Managing 5-50 client sites. Spending $5k-$20k/mo on tools and labor. Looking for automation that actually works, not another SaaS promise.

## What They Want
- ROI math: "replace $X/mo of labor with $Y/mo of agents"
- WordPress-specific: mumcp, SitePilotAI, Elementor automation
- Client management: multi-tenant, isolated credentials, per-client reporting
- Risk reduction: "what if the agent breaks something?" → governance, human gates

## How to Talk to Them
- Business language with technical proof
- Before/after case studies with dollar amounts
- "Your team manages 20 sites. Our agents manage 200."
- Address the fear: show governance tiers, approval flows, rollback capability
