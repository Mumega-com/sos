# LinkedIn

## Platform DNA
- Sweet spot: 1,300-2,000 characters
- Vibe: professional but human. Hadi's personal narrative + business insight
- Audience: [[builders]] + [[agencies]] — decision-makers with budgets

## Content Rules
- Lead with personal hook: "3 months ago I..." / "I was wrong about..."
- First 210 chars = everything (before "see more" truncation)
- Use [[hooks]] — proof hooks and behind-the-scenes perform best
- Match [[brand-voice]] but more professional. See [[platform-tone]]
- Short paragraphs, white space. links in first comment
- Business framing: costs saved, efficiency gained, team structure

## Formats
1. Personal Narrative — "I [built/learned/failed at something]" → lesson → takeaway
2. Case Study — "How we went from [before] to [after]" with real numbers
3. System Reveal — "Here's the architecture running my 19-agent team"
4. Contrarian — challenge conventional wisdom about AI agencies

## Posting: 3-5x/week, Mon-Thu. Peak: 12:00, 17:00 UTC
## Audience: [[builders]] + [[agencies]] — they want ROI and business impact
