## Decision

**loom** takes the squad PM seat for the Mumega Launch sprint. Loom is
*also* the SOS microkernel surface owner, but this decision adds the
coordination responsibility on top.

Two distinct "PM" roles exist in this squad — do not conflate:

| Role                 | Holder | Scope                                            |
| -------------------- | ------ | ------------------------------------------------ |
| **Squad PM**         | loom   | sprint cadence, unblocking peers, escalation     |
| **Mumega PM** (product) | codex  | mumega.com storefront copy + marketplace + brand |

## Rationale

Hadi's framing:

> "You are project manager of mumega launch because you can fix SOS."

The leverage is specifically that the PM can also *close* substrate
defects when they block the sprint (bus envelope, mesh gaps, etc.) —
not just coordinate around them. Separation of concerns would force a
handoff loop every time a SOS-side blocker appeared; collapsing coord
+ substrate authority removes that loop for the 9-day closure sprint.

Post-v1.0 freeze, this collapses back apart — codex stays mumega-PM,
loom goes back to maintenance.

## Sprint cadence

- **Morning** (any time Hadi is online) — Squad PM broadcasts state: what's done, what's blocked, what's in flight
- **Silent acks are fine** — no acknowledgement required for FYI broadcasts; ack only when explicitly asked
- **24h ack window** — if a direct ask to a peer goes unanswered for 24h, PM re-sends once via Discord, then escalates to Hadi
- **Decisions land as files** — every material decision goes in `sos/squads/mumega-launch/decisions/NNN-<slug>.md` with clear Why + rollback
- **Git-backed memory** — use `sos/squads/mumega-launch/memory/` for state that future sessions need; use `handoffs/` for peer handoffs

## What the PM does NOT do

- Make product decisions on mumega.com — that's codex's surface
- Touch prod CF routes or wrangler deploys without hermes's ack — that's the surface-ownership rule the squad exists to enforce
- Deploy Inkwell template changes — that's kasra's surface
