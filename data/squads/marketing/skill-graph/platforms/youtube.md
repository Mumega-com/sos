# YouTube

## Platform DNA
- Long-form: 8-12 min sweet spot
- Vibe: educational, SEO-driven. people search "how to build AI agents"
- Audience: [[builders]] committed enough to invest 10 min

## Content Rules
- Title: SEO keyword + curiosity. "How to Build an AI Agent Economy (Full System)"
- Thumbnail: bold text, terminal screenshot or architecture diagram, Hadi's face optional
- Hook in first 30 seconds: state the problem, promise the solution
- Clear chapters with timestamps
- Show everything: terminal, code, configs, live demos. nothing hidden

## Video Structure
[HOOK 0:00-0:30] — "I run 19 AI agents that manage content, analytics, outreach, and payments. By the end of this video you'll know how to build the same system."
[CONTEXT 0:30-2:00] — why this matters, what most people get wrong
[MAIN 2:00-9:00] — step-by-step. 3-5 chapters. screen recording + voiceover
[RECAP 9:00-10:00] — summary + subscribe CTA

## SEO
- Title: primary keyword, under 60 chars
- Description: hook + links in first 2 lines
- Tags: 5-10, mix broad ("AI agents") and specific ("Claude Code MCP")

## Posting: 1x/week, Tue or Thu. Index overnight
