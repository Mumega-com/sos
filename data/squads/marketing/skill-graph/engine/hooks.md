# Hook Formulas

Use these to open every post. Match hook type to platform and topic.
See [[platform-tone]] for delivery adjustments.

## The Proof Hook
"[Before] → [after] in [timeframe]"
- Best on: X, LinkedIn
- Examples:
  - "$8k/mo content spend → $15/mo after building 5 AI agents"
  - "0 agents → 19 agents running a complete economy in 3 months"
  - "manual WordPress updates → 239 MCP tools doing it autonomously"

## The Contrarian Hook
"You don't need [thing everyone assumes]. You need [our approach]."
- Best on: X, Threads
- Examples:
  - "you don't need a content team. you need a content organism."
  - "you don't need 10 SaaS tools. you need 10 agents on a bus."
  - "AI agents don't need frameworks. they need a kernel and a bus."

## The Replacement Hook
"I replaced [expensive thing] with [our thing]"
- Best on: X, TikTok, Instagram slide 1
- Examples:
  - "I replaced my entire content team with 5 agents for $15/month"
  - "we replaced manual analytics with a flywheel that learns from its own results"
  - "we replaced Zapier with a bus that routes messages between 19 AI agents"

## The Discovery Hook
"we just built/found/shipped [thing]"
- Best on: X, newsletter
- Examples:
  - "we just wired a complete economy in one session — Stripe to Solana"
  - "we just shipped a CMS in one Claude Code session"
  - "an agent just fixed 11 bugs without a human writing code"

## The Behind-the-Scenes Hook
"here's what [impressive thing] actually looks like inside"
- Best on: X, LinkedIn, YouTube
- Examples:
  - "here's what 19 AI agents working together actually looks like (terminal recording)"
  - "here's what a $15/month marketing team produces in a week"

## The Architecture Hook
"the system that [does impressive thing] fits in [small number] of files"
- Best on: X threads, YouTube, blog
- Examples:
  - "our entire agent economy runs on 7 wires. here's each one:"
  - "14 files define how 19 agents coordinate. the math is the manager."

## The Philosophy Hook
"[deep observation about AI/work/economy]"
- Best on: Threads, newsletter, LinkedIn
- Examples:
  - "agents don't need motivation. they need conductance."
  - "the future of work isn't AI replacing humans. it's humans and AI sharing an economy."

## Rules
- Test 2-3 hooks per topic before publishing
- Track which types get engagement PER PLATFORM
- Update weekly: remove underperformers, add winners from real data
- The hook for X is almost never the same as the hook for LinkedIn
