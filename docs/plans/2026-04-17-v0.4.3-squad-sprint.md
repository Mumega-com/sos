## Why a new specialist

The v0.4.0 squad covers schemas, models, OpenAPIs, tests. Brain v0.4.3 also needs **in-service handler wiring** — a different shape of work (mutate `BrainService`, maintain `BrainState`, emit bus events). A narrow specialist with the Brain file tree and the bus consumer pattern baked into its system prompt will be crisper than a generic `Agent` call. Scoped to v0.4.3; may be retired at v0.5.0.
